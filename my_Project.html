Tony Elumelu:

Onyemachi Tony elumelu is an entrepreneur and the founder of elumelu foundations. he has become a wave inspiring young people world_wide

Elumelu was born in Jos, Plateau, Nigeria, on 22 March 1963

to Suzanne and Dominic Elumelu from Onicha-Ukwu in Aniocha North Local Government Area of Delta State.

He grew up with four siblings, one of whom is Ndudi Elumelu, a minority leader in the Nigerian Federal House of Representatives.

Holdings:

He became the chairman of Heirs holdings in 2010 and He is also the Chairman of Transcorp he is a chair bord member at UBA(Uinited Bank of Africa)

Family:

Elumelu married Awele Vivien Elumelu, a medical doctor. They have seven(7) children together.

Education:

He studied Economics at Bendel State University (now Ambrose Alli University) obtaining a Bachelor of Science.

Afterward, he studied and received a Master of Science degree in Economics at the University of Lagos

Tony elumelu has been involved in several philantropical activities and he remains a global figure till today
